# Assets Folder

## Images
Place your project images in the `images/` folder:
- `project1.jpg` - Your first project screenshot/image
- `project2.jpg` - Your second project screenshot/image
- `project3.jpg` - Your third project screenshot/image

Recommended image size: 1200x675 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio)

## PDFs
Place your resume PDF in the `pdfs/` folder:
- `resume.pdf` - Your resume document

The resume will be viewable and downloadable on the /resume page.
